Company accessibility from anywhere in the world

Having our cloud-based solution for your manufacturing management boosts efficiency and effectiveness. For this niche of business, your company can access data from anywhere in the world and at anytime with real-time data from all teams.

You also get quality automation on two things: procurement and order management. Production can be supervised from all ends and your multiple manufacturing procedures receive adequate support.

Fastrasuite supports these multiple manufacturing procedures

Well-structured Dashboard

The dedicated dashboard of the manufacturing management module.

Run complete production operations of your manufacturing unit effectively and grow your business with a secure, scalable, and future-proof cloud platform

Complete production operations

Run complete production operations of your manufacturing unit effectively from the initial stages of planning to the deployment of the completed products to the inventory. The dedicated manufacturing module along with the associated modules will help you to manage the production operations of the various products.

Furthermore, you can do the planning aspects on the manufacturing operations involving the engineering side of it with the integration with advanced external or third pastry tools of operation.

Dedicated work center overview

A complete informational menu on the work center operations of the manufacturing process; Workstation classification, Plan work orders directly, Dedicated work center reports, Overall equipment effectiveness

Planning operations

Plan your manufacturing and production operations for complete effectiveness in operations; Master production schedule window, Plan by production, Dedicated planning for each work center, Run scheduler option

Dedicated maintenance module

Run your manufacturing equipment as well as the company equipment maintenance with the dedicated maintenance management module such as; Run preventive as well as predictive maintenance, Scheduled maintenance operations, Automate preventive maintenance schedule, Generate maintenance request

Dedicated repoting

Report generation is one of the advanced capabilities with Fastra and for the manufacturing operations, you will be able to generate dedicated analytical reports on each of the operations. such as; Manufacturing order reports, Work order report, Overall equipment effectiveness report
